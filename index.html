Our Key Services

We offer a range of environmental services, specializing in vegetation management, hazardous waste remediation, civil construction, landscaping and hardscaping. We help clients with projects like wildfire debris removal, stream restoration, managing vegetation around critical infrastructure, and creating beautiful, functional outdoor spaces through professional landscaping and hardscaping solutions for public and private sector clients.

  • Vegetation Management: Controls overgrowth, reduces wildfire hazards, and maintains clearance for utilities through services like pre- and post-inspections.
  • Landscaping & Hardscaping: Professional design, installation, and construction of landscapes and hardscapes. We create attractive, durable outdoor environments including patios, retaining walls, pathways, planting beds, and integrated tree/vegetation features for residential, commercial, and municipal properties across Michigan.
  • Remediation and Cleanup: Cleans up contaminated land and water, handles hazardous waste, and removes debris following events like wildfires.
  • Construction: Includes civil construction, site preparation, and structural demolition.
  • Environmental Engineering: Provides technical assistance, project design, permitting, and consultation from project conception to completion.
  • Stream and Waterway Work: Engages in stream restoration and designs natural stream channels.
  • Tree Diagnosis and Treatment: Expert specialization in identifying and treating tree health issues, ensuring long-term vitality.
  • Municipal Contractual Services: Available for hire on contracts requiring an ISA Certified Arborist for municipalities, colonies, new construction, preservation, and more.
  • Regenerative Agriculture Consulting: Leveraging a bachelor's degree in regenerative agriculture and sustainability, we offer expert guidance on sustainable farming practices, soil health improvement, crop rotation, and ecosystem restoration to promote long-term land productivity and environmental health.
  • Sustainability Consulting: Providing comprehensive sustainability strategies, including senior project-based insights on eco-friendly initiatives, resource management, and sustainable development for municipal, agricultural, and construction projects in Michigan's exploding sustainability sector.
