Our Key Services

We offer a range of environmental services, specializing in vegetation management, hazardous waste remediation, civil construction, landscaping, and hardscaping. We help clients with projects like wildfire debris removal, stream restoration, and managing vegetation around critical infrastructure.

  • Vegetation Management: Controls overgrowth, reduces wildfire hazards, and maintains clearance for utilities through pre- and post-inspections.
  • Landscaping & Hardscaping: Professional landscaping design/installation and hardscaping solutions tailored to Michigan properties, new construction, and municipal projects.
  • Remediation and Cleanup: Cleans up contaminated land and water, handles hazardous waste, and removes debris following events like wildfires.
  • Construction: Civil construction, site preparation, and structural demolition.
  • Environmental Engineering: Technical assistance, project design, permitting, and consultation from conception to completion.
  • Stream and Waterway Work: Stream restoration and natural stream channel design.
  • Tree Diagnosis and Treatment: Expert identification and treatment of tree health issues for long-term vitality.
  • Municipal Contractual Services: ISA Certified Arborist for municipalities, colonies, new construction, and preservation.
  • Regenerative Agriculture Consulting: Guidance on sustainable farming, soil health, crop rotation, and ecosystem restoration.
